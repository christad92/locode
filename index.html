What is

Locode is a unique addressing solution that is aimed at solving the problems around addressing, geocoding and location in Nigeria by leveraging on the existing systems. It focuses on how to make addresses machine readable and bringing social interactions into addressing and location intelligence around the world.

The locode addresses will work in any where in the world saving people from having to type long addresses that most computers cannot read or geocode as well as saving them from explaining complex addresses in Nigeria and other emerging markets. With locode business will find customers and potential customers will find the right service providers based on location and other recommendation systems built into our AI system.

Why

Locode will cause a disrupt change in how businesses that rely on addresses and supply chain information will operate and scale their operations. Helping business and customers to meet at no extra costs and eventually will change how all of us, at a subliminal level will think about addresses and not worry about getting to places in the absence of local knowledge.

Locode will empower public health systems and stakeholders to make informed decisions based on locations. Leveraging on patients addresses will help in understanding the epidemic phenomena and vulnerability on a precise local scale rather than the hypothetical regional scale.
